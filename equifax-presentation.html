WTP Formula Introduction

WTP = α(IQS × BCS) + β(ID) + γ(CES) + δ(TS)

Where:

•IQS: Insight Quality Score — Measures accuracy, timeliness, and depth of analytics insights

•BCS: Bundle Coverage Score — Breadth of use cases covered (credit, fraud, identity, analytics)

•ID: Integration Depth — Degree of API/workflow embedding within client systems

•CES: Compliance Ease Score — Simplicity and completeness of regulatory compliance support

•TS: Trust Score — Brand perception, security confidence, and reliability metrics

•Weights α, β, γ, δ — Calibrated through customer research and pricing experiments to reflect segment-specific value drivers
